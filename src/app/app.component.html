Competitive Matchmaking

Prowess Matchmaking presents: The Command Hub. The Command Hub is a new data tracking software designed specifically for cEDH (competitive commander) players and Tournament Organizers. On the Command Hub, you'll be able to check out all sorts of relevant data, such as different deck usages, win rates, best opening hands, etc. In addition to general data, you'll be able to track your own statistics. For example, you can see what players and what decks you tend to struggle against, and how other players that use similar decks overcome your bad match ups. The Command Hub will be your all-in-one spot for cEDH stats and personal improvement.
